Privacy Policy — cheatXtwitter

Last updated: June 2026

cheatXtwitter is a Chrome extension that helps you draft posts and replies for X (Twitter) using AI. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account data — if you sign in (email, Google, Telegram, or X), we store your account identifier and display name on our server to provide the service.
  • Content you provide — text, images, video, or audio you add as sources, and chat messages, are sent to our API or your chosen AI provider to generate posts.
  • Usage — anonymous client ID and request counts for rate limiting (no cross-site tracking).

Local processing

Video/audio transcription can run locally in your browser (Whisper via WebAssembly). Transcripts may then be sent to the AI service you select.

Third parties

  • Our hosted API (Render) — authentication and shared AI when you use the default assistant.
  • AI providers — only if you enable your own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, etc.).
  • X (Twitter) — only when you use features on x.com; we do not post on your behalf without your action.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not auto-publish posts or replies — you review and publish on X yourself.

Data retention

Chat history and settings are stored locally in your browser. Server-side account data is kept while your account is active.

Contact

Questions: use the support contact listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
